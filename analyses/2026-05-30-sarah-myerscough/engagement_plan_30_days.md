## WHAT NOT TO DO (RED FLAGS TO AVOID)

### ❌ NEVER:
- Send cold pitch DMs to galleries
- Ask for portfolio review in comments
- Comment "Beautiful work!" with no substance
- Like every single post (looks bot-like)
- Tag galleries in your work without invitation
- Comment on posts older than 2 weeks
- Argue or critique in comments
- Talk about follower counts or engagement metrics
- Use hashtags like #craft #woodworking in comments
- Comment more than 2x per week on same account

### ⚠️ BE CAREFUL:
- Don't over-share personal struggles or challenges
- Don't mention pricing or sales in comments
- Don't name-drop other galleries in comments
- Don't compare artists to each other
- Don't use overly academic language
- Don't write essay-length comments (3 sentences max)

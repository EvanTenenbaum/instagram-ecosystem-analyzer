### Phase 2: Content Shift (Weeks 3-6)

1. **Increase posting frequency:** From 0.3/week to 2-3/week minimum
2. **Shift timing:** Test Sunday afternoon and Tuesday morning posts
3. **Content ratio:** 50% process (lathe, chisel, material), 30% finished work, 20% narrative/studio
4. **Drop hobby hashtags:** Remove #woodworking, #handmade, #woodturning (signals hobbyist)
5. **Add craft hashtags:** #contemporarycraft, #collectibledesign, #woodart, #materiality
6. **Target:** Increase commenters/post from 3.9 to 6.0+
